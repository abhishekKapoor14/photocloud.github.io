Hey Folks.. wanna be a good photographer?

Every photographer discovers their passion in a different way. Maybe you started snapping photos with a toy camera as a child, or maybe you accidentally fell into photography when you started photographing your own children. However you started the journey, it’s important to feed your inspiration so you don’t lose that joy.

“If I have any ‘message’ worth giving to a beginner it is that there are no shortcuts in photography.” – Edward Weston
